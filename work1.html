An online vehicle recycle App

Project type: University Mini Project

Role: UX/UI designer

Industry: Automobile

Case Study: UX Research for Online Old Vehicle Recycle App

Overview

The project aimed to create an online platform facilitating the recycling of old vehicles and enabling the purchase of recycled car parts. The app included Admin, Customer, and Staff interfaces, focusing on streamlining vehicle recycling and enhancing the car parts purchasing experience.

Research Objectives

  • Understand User Needs: Identify pain points and preferences of users interested in vehicle recycling and purchasing recycled parts.
  • Evaluate User Interface: Assess the usability of Admin, Customer, and Staff interfaces to ensure intuitive navigation.
  • Gather Feedback: Obtain user insights to enhance existing features and propose improvements.

Methodology

  1. User Interviews: Conducted semi-structured interviews with vehicle owners and potential buyers to explore motivations and challenges.
  2. Surveys: Distributed online surveys to gather quantitative data on user demographics, preferences, and satisfaction levels.
  3. Analysis: Evaluated competitors' platforms to benchmark features, user interfaces, and customer feedback.

Findings

  • User Needs: Users preferred a straightforward process for selling vehicles and desired detailed product descriptions when purchasing car parts.
  • Pain Points: Challenges included complex vehicle submission forms, opaque pricing calculations, and limited product information.
  • Insights for Improvement: Recommendations included implementing a step-by-step vehicle submission process, enhancing product descriptions, and improving customer support responsiveness.
